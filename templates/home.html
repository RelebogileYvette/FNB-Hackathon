How to Hire

  1. Create a job post, detailing the requirements and specifications of your project.
  2. Get proposals from freelancers, who will build a sample of your project for you to review. You can choose the style you like the most, or select a freelancer directly to create a booking with them.
  3. An agile plan can be created for you, or you can upload your own plan. The plan will be accessible to the freelancer you approve. It will also go through a review process, and you can use the platform to attend online meetings for code progress reviews or other discussions based on the proposed plan.

Payment: You'll only pay the freelancer you authorize.
